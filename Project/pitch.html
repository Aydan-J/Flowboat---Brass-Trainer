🎯 Chromatic Tuner

Start your mic and play any note — the tuner will detect it and show you how close you are.

💡 How It Works
  1. Click Start Listening and allow mic access.
  2. Play any note on your instrument.
  3. The tuner detects the closest note and shows how many cents off you are.
  4. 🟢 Green = in tune · 🟡 Yellow = slightly off · 🔴 Red = sharp or flat
